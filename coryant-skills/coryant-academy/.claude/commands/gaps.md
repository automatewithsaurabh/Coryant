Brief: $ARGUMENTS

Run GAP_FINDER moment type. Before identifying gaps, use web search to confirm what exists:
- Search the exact topic AND variant terminology (gaps often exist under a different name)
- Search adjacent disciplines (the gap may be filled there)
- Search recent preprints (the gap may be being filled right now)

Produce:
- Gap confirmation check: what was searched, what was found, any near-misses
- Genuine gaps identified (2–3): type, description, why it's a real gap, feasibility, significance, one-sentence contribution
- Near-misses: papers that are close but genuinely different — with the distinction the researcher can use
- False gaps: any "gaps" that already exist in the literature (cite specifically)
- Positioning recommendation: strongest gap, theoretical framework, prior literature to engage, one-sentence contribution

A claimed gap is only real if it survives a serious search. The search must precede the claim.
