Draft a professional engagement letter for the following CA mandate:
$ARGUMENTS

Use the engagement letter structure from `agents/client-brief.md`:
1. Scope of Services (specific bullets — what is included)
2. Services Excluded (what is NOT covered)
3. Fee Arrangement (fixed / time-based / retainer — note what needs to be filled in)
4. Client Responsibilities (what information they must provide and when)
5. Timing (expected timeline)
6. Confidentiality and Data Protection
7. Professional Standards (ICAI / ICAEW / relevant body)
8. Termination
9. Liability Limitation if applicable
10. Signature Block

Rules:
- Scope must be specific. Vague scope creates disputes.
- Exclusions must be explicit. If it's not in scope, say so.
- If fee, timeline, or scope details are missing from the brief, note them clearly as "TBC: [what needs to be decided]" — do not invent them.
- Plain English throughout — no Latin phrases.
- Produce a complete draft ready to review and send.
