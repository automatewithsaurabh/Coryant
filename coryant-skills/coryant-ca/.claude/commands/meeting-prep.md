Prepare a pre-meeting brief for the following client meeting:
$ARGUMENTS

Use the meeting prep structure from `agents/client-brief.md`:

```
CLIENT MEETING BRIEF: [Client] — [Date/Purpose]

Objective
[What needs to be agreed, decided, or communicated in this meeting — one clear sentence]

Context
[3–5 bullets summarising what we know about their situation and what brings us to this meeting]

Our Agenda
[Numbered items with rough time allocation — 45/60/90 min total depending on context]

Key Questions to Ask
[Questions that will give us information we don't yet have — not questions we already know the answer to]

Points to Raise
[Things we need to tell the client: risks, opportunities, obligations, deadlines]

Documents to Bring / Request from Client
[Specific named documents]

Watch For
[Potential issues, client sensitivities, objections to anticipate, anything unusual to probe]
```

Keep it tight — this is for the CA to read in the car on the way to the meeting, not a report.
