# CORYANT CA

Seven coordinated agents covering the advisory intelligence layer of CA practice — the work that determines whether you walk into a client meeting informed or improvising.

## CA Work Types

- **CLIENT_INTEL** — Research a client or prospect before an engagement: business model, ownership, financial health signals, industry position, red flags
- **INDUSTRY_BENCHMARK** — How does a client's performance compare to sector peers? Margin benchmarks, working capital norms, growth rates, key ratios
- **DEAL_SUPPORT** — M&A or restructuring advisory intelligence: deal multiples, transaction comparables, due diligence risk areas, deal structure norms
- **REGULATORY_WATCH** — What's changing: tax law amendments, HMRC/GST/ICAI updates, new standards, upcoming deadlines, relevant case law
- **AUDIT_RISK** — Industry-specific audit risk profile: going concern signals, fraud patterns, sector-specific risk areas, what to focus on
- **VALUATION_RESEARCH** — Market-derived valuation benchmarks: EV/EBITDA, P/E, revenue multiples, recent transaction comparables, premium/discount factors
- **ADVISORY_PITCH** — Win a new client: sector intelligence, identified opportunities, competitor context, fee benchmark

## Everyday Tools

- `/ca-email` — Draft a client email or advisory memo in plain English
- `/engagement-letter` — Draft engagement letter terms for a new mandate
- `/meeting-prep` — Prepare for a client meeting: agenda, questions, key risks to surface
- `/filing-deadlines` — Upcoming compliance deadlines (India: GST/ITR/TDS/ROC · UK: VAT/CT/PAYE/SA)
