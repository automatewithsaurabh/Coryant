$ARGUMENTS

Requirements:
- Produce a specific, usable calendar — not "post about X topic" but "post: [specific title/angle/hook] on [platform] in [format]"
- Each entry: Date | Platform | Format | Topic/Angle | Hook (first line) | Goal (what this piece accomplishes)
- The calendar should have a logical arc: it's not random topics, it builds something over the period
- Week 1–2: establish the core positioning/point of view
- Week 3–6: own the primary topic territory
- Week 7+: expand into adjacent topics and repurpose what worked
- Mix of formats: not every post the same type
- Flag 2–3 "anchor pieces" — the highest-effort, highest-impact content that the lighter pieces orbit

If 90 days is requested: provide full detail for weeks 1–4, outline for weeks 5–8, direction for weeks 9–12.

If posting frequency is not specified: recommend the minimum viable frequency for this platform and niche that builds real momentum.

Produce as a clean table, then add a brief strategic note per week.
