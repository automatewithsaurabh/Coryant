# CORYANT CONTENT

Eight coordinated agents covering the full content stack — from knowing exactly who your audience is and what they want, to producing content that earns attention.

## Content Moment Types

- **STRATEGY_BUILD** — Build a content strategy from scratch: what to create, for whom, on which channels, at what frequency
- **COMPETITOR_CONTENT** — What are competitors publishing? What's getting traction? What topics do they dominate and where are the gaps?
- **AUDIENCE_INTEL** — Who exactly is your audience? Pain points, language patterns, communities, what they share, who they trust
- **TREND_HUNT** — What topics are emerging *right now* before they peak? The content opportunity window — find it before competitors do
- **CHANNEL_STRATEGY** — Which platform is worth your time for this niche and audience? LinkedIn vs YouTube vs newsletter vs SEO vs podcast
- **SEO_RESEARCH** — Keyword clusters, search intent, difficulty, featured snippet opportunities, People Also Ask, content gaps no one has filled
- **VIRAL_ANATOMY** — Dissect why specific content goes viral in this niche: hook patterns, structure, emotional triggers, format signals
- **CONTENT_AUDIT** — What is your existing content doing? What's earning attention, what's dead weight, what to double down on
