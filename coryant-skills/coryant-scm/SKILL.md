# CORYANT Supply Chain — Strategic Intelligence Pack

## What This Is

On-demand strategic intelligence for procurement managers, supply chain strategists, and operations leaders who make sourcing, supplier, and supply network decisions without a large analyst team.

This pack covers the **strategic intelligence layer** — the research and analysis that informs decisions. It does not replace real-time operational tools (live freight rates, live port status). When market data is time-sensitive, outputs are evidence-graded and flagged for verification.

---

## 8 Moment Types

### SUPPLIER_INTEL
Research a specific supplier before onboarding, during audit, or ahead of renegotiation. Financial health, ownership structure, capacity, certifications, compliance history, red flags.

### SUPPLIER_RISK
Assess exposure to a specific supplier or supplier base. Geopolitical risk, single-source dependency, financial distress signals, concentration risk, climate/operational exposure.

### TARIFF_TRADE_WATCH
Understand the trade rules affecting a sourcing decision. US tariff schedules (HTS), EU customs, India DGFT, free trade agreements, sanctions (OFAC/EU/UK), rules of origin, country-of-origin strategies.

### ALTERNATIVE_SOURCING
Map the supplier landscape for a category or component. Identify alternative suppliers by geography, capability, and cost profile. Country-of-origin comparison for sourcing strategy.

### MARKET_BENCHMARK
Provide market context for a procurement decision. Commodity price trends and volatility, freight rate benchmarks by route and mode, category spend benchmarks, should-cost reference data.

### DISRUPTION_SCAN
Early warning scan for supply chain disruptions in a region, route, or category. Geopolitical events, port congestion, labor disputes, climate risks, capacity constraints, single-event risks.

### NEGOTIATION_PREP
Prepare for a supplier negotiation. Should-cost modelling, supplier leverage assessment, alternative count, market price benchmarks, walk-away position, concession hierarchy.

### NETWORK_INTEL
Strategic analysis for supply network decisions. Country risk profiles for manufacturing and sourcing, nearshoring/reshoring analysis, landed cost comparison by route, logistics capability by region.

## Output Formats

**INTEL** — Full HTML intelligence report with all sections, evidence grades, and adversarial review. For strategic decisions with time to review.

**BRIEF** — Concise decision brief (1–2 pages). For fast pre-meeting or pre-call context.

**BOTH** — Full report + executive summary brief.

---

## Quality Standards

- Every load-bearing claim carries an evidence grade: `[A]` primary/verified · `[B]` single source · `[C]` inferred or historical
- Market data (commodity prices, freight rates, tariff rates) always graded `[B]` or `[C]` with a verification instruction
- Adversarial reviewer (Stage 3.5) challenges every top conclusion before synthesis
- Geopolitical and regulatory intelligence is time-stamped; stale intel is flagged
- Personal names never appear in output
