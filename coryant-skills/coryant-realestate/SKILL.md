# CORYANT Real Estate

**Decision-grade intelligence for property investors, developers, and commercial landlords.**

This pack exists because the real estate industry has plenty of data tools and zero decision tools. CoStar tells you the cap rate. Rightmove shows you the comparable. Nobody tells you whether to buy.

This pack tells you whether to buy.

---

## The Principle

Every output from this pack opens with a staked verdict: **BUY / PASS / NEGOTIATE** — with specific terms.

Before any positive analysis runs, a mandatory red flag scan looks for reasons NOT to do the deal. That is how a good surveyor, solicitor, or underwriter thinks. It is the opposite of how vendor marketing is structured.

### Investor Lane

## What This Pack Does NOT Do

- **Live transaction data** — Comparables are sourced from public data and web search, not real-time MLS, CoStar, or Land Registry feeds. Verify prices before relying on them for negotiations.
- **Professional valuation** — Output from this pack is analytical intelligence, not an RICS Red Book valuation, USPAP appraisal, or equivalent. Do not use it for mortgage or financing purposes without independent professional valuation.
- **Legal advice** — Planning assessments and lease analysis are intelligence, not legal advice. Instruct a solicitor for transactional matters.
- **Live planning portals** — Planning histories require verification via the local planning authority portal.
- **Construction quotes** — Cost benchmarks are reference data, not quotes. Obtain contractor pricing before proceeding with development decisions.

## Geography

Primary coverage — UK, US, India, Europe. Every output specifies which jurisdiction's frameworks apply.

| Jurisdiction | Standards Applied |
|-------------|------------------|
| UK | RICS Red Book, SDLT, NPPF planning, use classes, freehold/leasehold |
| US | Cap rate/NOI conventions, 1031 exchange, zoning ordinances, USPAP |
| India | RERA, circle rates, state stamp duty, FSI/FAR, DCR |
| Europe | Net initial yields, ERV, institutional lease standards, GDPR |

## Evidence Grading

Every load-bearing claim carries a source grade:

| Grade | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| `[A]` | Public record — Land Registry, Companies House, government planning portal |
| `[B]` | Verified market data — published comparable, rated data provider |
| `[C]` | Estimated / synthesised — stated assumptions, require independent verification |

All financial model inputs are `[C]` by default and must be stated as assumptions. Exit yield is always the most sensitive input.
