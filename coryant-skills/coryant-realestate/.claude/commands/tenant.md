Read agents/tenant-covenant.md, agents/lease-analyser.md. Read mode-intel.md.

Tenant: $ARGUMENTS

Run TENANT_COVENANT moment type.

Step 1: Establish the correct legal entity — the trading company, not the brand name. Search Companies House (UK) / SEC / MCA (India) for the registered entity that signed the lease.

Step 2: Use web search to find most recent financial data:
- Annual report / accounts
- Revenue and profitability trend
- Any distress signals: CVA, administration, going concern, late filing, restructuring news
- Sector outlook

Step 3: Assess:
- Financial health (revenue, profitability, leverage, liquidity)
- Sector risk (structural headwinds or tailwinds)
- Break probability (economic test + business condition)
- Renewal probability
- Void cost if tenant vacates (rates, dilapidations, re-letting period, incentives)

Output:
- Covenant rating: STRONG / ADEQUATE / WEAK / SPECULATIVE / UNVERIFIABLE
- Specific recommendation: is this covenant acceptable at the acquisition yield? If not, what security is required (rent deposit, parent guarantee, pricing discount)?
