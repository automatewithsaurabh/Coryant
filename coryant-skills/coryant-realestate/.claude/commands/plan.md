Site: $ARGUMENTS

Run PLANNING_VIABILITY moment type.

Use web search to check:
- Planning portal for this specific address: existing applications, decisions, enforcement notices
- Listed building / conservation area status (UK: Historic England; US: National Register)
- Local Plan allocation for the site
- Any Article 4 Directions removing permitted development rights (UK)
- Flood risk constraints
- Environmental designations

Produce:
- Current lawful use and applicable use class/zoning
- What is permitted by right (PD/by-right zoning) vs. what requires full consent
- Planning history summary (notable decisions and what they establish)
- Proposed development assessment: policy support or conflict, key design/planning considerations
- Planning risk: VERY LOW / LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH / VERY HIGH — with specific evidence
- Strategy: what to do first (PD prior approval / pre-app / full application / alternative use), realistic timeline, CIL/S106 obligations estimate

Never state that planning is certain. State probabilities and strategy.
