# CORYANT SALES

Eight coordinated agents covering the full sales intelligence stack — from knowing which accounts to target and why, to walking into every call prepared, every deal coached, and every competitive situation with a specific strategy.

## Sales Moment Types

- **ACCOUNT_INTEL** — Deep research on a target account: current initiatives, trigger events, org structure, tech stack, buying signals, financials, why NOW is the right time
- **PROSPECT_RESEARCH** — Individual prospect intelligence: mandate, career trajectory, what they care about, why they'd be receptive to outreach right now
- **OUTREACH_BUILD** — Personalised cold outreach built on real account and prospect research — email, LinkedIn, call script
- **DEAL_REVIEW** — Honest deal health assessment: MEDDPICC scoring, champion strength, risk diagnosis, specific next actions
- **COMPETITIVE_BATTLE** — Win against a specific competitor in a live deal: their real weaknesses, FUD to counter, proof points to deploy, traps to avoid
- **OBJECTION_INTEL** — Handle specific objections: is it real or a deflection, what's the right response, what's worked for others in the same situation
- **ICP_INTEL** — Which accounts to prioritise in a territory and why: firmographic fit, technographic signals, intent data, trigger events
- **NEGOTIATION_PREP** — Prepare for commercial negotiation: BATNA, anchor strategy, what to give vs. what to get, where to hold firm

## Everyday Tools

- `/research` — quick account + prospect brief before any call
- `/outreach` — personalised cold email or LinkedIn message
- `/battle` — quick competitive battlecard for a specific deal
- `/call-prep` — pre-call brief with questions, objections, talk tracks
- `/deal-check` — honest deal health score with specific next actions
- `/follow-up` — follow-up email after a meeting or call
- `/sequence` — multi-touch outreach sequence (email + LinkedIn + call)
